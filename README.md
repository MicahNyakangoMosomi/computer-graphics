## Design Overview:
The house is divided into three main zones:
### Public Zone
Entrance, Living Room
### Semi-Public Zone
Dining Room, Kitchen,
### Private Zone
Master Bedroom, Bedroom 1, Bedroom 2, Bathrooms
## Entrance & Living Room
- The main entrance opens directly into the living room
- The living room acts as a central gathering space
- It provides access to:
Bedrooms,Extra room,Bathroom
This placement prevents visitors from passing through private areas
## Dining Room Placement
- The dining room is centrally located
- It connects the living room and bedroom areas
- Acts as a transition space between private and public zones
- Reduces direct wall-sharing between bedrooms, improving acoustic comfort

## Kitchen Design
- The kitchen is positioned on the far right of the plan
- Close proximity to:
Bathroom, 
Plumbing fixtures Including:
-- Sink
-- Floor Drain (FD)
This reduces pipe length and construction costs
